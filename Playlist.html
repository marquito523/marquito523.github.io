ABOUT THIS FEATURE

ExFrame Playlists is a unique system on Discord. You don't want to buy Spotify Prenium although you want to listen to music freely without having any advertisements ? Well ExFrame Playlists can now solve your problem. Any music you want you can play on ExFrame.

◉ Play other peoples' playlists
◉ Create up to 25 Playlists for free and 75 if you own Prenium
◉ Play your own playlist
◉ Unlimited song amount per playlist.
